1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

We may collect the following categories of information from users of the Service.

When you create an account or interact with our platform, we may collect personal information including:

  • Full name
  • Email address

When you access the Service, we may automatically collect certain technical data, including:

  • IP address
  • Device identifiers
  • Operating system and device type
  • Browser type
  • App usage statistics
  • Viewing activity and watch history
  • Time spent viewing content
  • Referral URLs
  • Log files and crash reports

This information helps us maintain system performance and improve the user experience.

C. Location Information

Vertyz may collect approximate geolocation data derived from:

  • IP address
  • Device settings
  • Network signals

This data may be used to:

  • Comply with geographic licensing restrictions
  • Personalize content recommendations
  • Improve advertising relevance

Vertyz does not collect precise GPS location without explicit user permission.

D. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Vertyz uses cookies, SDKs, and similar tracking technologies to enhance user experience and analyze platform performance.

These may include:

  • Session cookies
  • Persistent cookies
  • Analytics tags
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Device fingerprinting technologies

Users may manage cookie preferences through their device or browser settings.
